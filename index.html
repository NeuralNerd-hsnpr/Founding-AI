Q3: What is your philosophy on "building vs. buying" when it comes to AI models and infrastructure?

My philosophy is rooted in maximizing competitive advantage while minimizing operational overhead: We should BUY commodity services, and BUILD proprietary competitive advantage.

Buy/Leverage (90% of Time)

For commodity, general intelligence tasks (summarization, general Q&A, classification), we should leverage robust, managed APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). This ensures SOTA performance and minimizes MLOps debt.

Build (10% of Time)

We only build custom models when the task is unique, proprietary, and directly generates revenue or informs a multi-million dollar decision. (e.g., The strategic M&A Model, the custom HVC predictive model).
